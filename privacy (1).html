Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 2026

Ask Arnold ("we," "us," or "our") provides AI-powered appointment scheduling communications on behalf of the service businesses that use our platform (each, a "Business"). This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you communicate with a Business through our SMS and voice service.

Information We Collect

When you text or call a phone number connected to Ask Arnold, we collect:

  • Your phone number
  • The content of your messages, including service requests and appointment preferences
  • Appointment details you provide, such as preferred dates, times, and service type

How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide solely to:

  • Schedule, confirm, reschedule, or cancel appointments with the Business you contacted
  • Send appointment reminders and related service communications
  • Improve the reliability and accuracy of our scheduling service

Mobile Information & Message Data

No mobile information will be shared with third parties or affiliates for marketing or promotional purposes. Information sharing with subcontractors in support services, such as customer service, is permitted for the sole purpose of operating Ask Arnold's scheduling service on behalf of the Business you contacted. All other use case categories, including sharing with third parties for their own marketing purposes, are excluded from this description and are not permitted under this policy.

Message Frequency & Rates

Message frequency varies depending on your appointment activity, typically 2 to 4 messages per booking (confirmation and reminders). Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying STOP to any message. Reply HELP for assistance.

Data Retention

We retain appointment-related information only as long as necessary to provide the scheduling service and to meet the record-keeping needs of the Business you contacted.

Contact

Questions about this policy can be sent to privacy@askarnold.ai.
